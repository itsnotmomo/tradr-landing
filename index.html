How It Works

Simple. Transparent.
Fully verified.

On Tradr, every trade is tracked from start to finish. Automatically. The market decides the outcome — not the trader. Here's how it works.

01 ——
📡
Post Your Trade
Pick your market, choose a direction (buy or sell), and set your entry price, stop loss, and take profit. The live price is pulled automatically — no way to fake it.
02 ——
🔍
We Watch the Market
Our system monitors the price around the clock using real market data. Every open trade is tracked live until it hits one of your levels.
03 ——
⚡
Win or Loss — Automatically
When the price hits your take profit or stop loss, the trade is marked as a WIN or LOSS. You can't edit it, delete it, or change it after the fact.
04 ——
📈
Your Record Builds
Every trade adds to your permanent track record. Your win rate, performance, and history — all verified, all public. Your results tell the story.
