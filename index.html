How It Works

Simple. Transparent.
Fully verified.

On Tradr, every trade is tracked from start to finish — automatically. The market decides the outcome. Not the trader. Here's how it works.

01 ——
📡
Post Your Trade
Pick your market, choose a direction, set your entry, stop loss, and take profit. The live price is pulled automatically — no way to fake it.
02 ——
🔍
We Watch the Market
Our system monitors price around the clock using real market data. Every open trade is tracked live until it hits your levels.
03 ——
⚡
Win or Loss — Automatic
When price hits your take profit or stop loss, the trade is marked WIN or LOSS. You can't edit it, delete it, or change it after the fact.
04 ——
📈
Your Record Builds
Every trade adds to your permanent track record. Win rate, performance, history — all verified, all public. Your results speak for themselves.
